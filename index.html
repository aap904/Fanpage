Fanpage : Dark Souls

Welcome to my fanpage.

Here you can find some information about my favorite video game series : Dark Souls

I like this game series for its originality and changes to the simple "knight vs supernatural beast" formula followed by games of this genre.

The gameplay is also very original, as it intertwines the player's actions makes into the overall environment

The games are also very challenging, which is something I enjoy in video games.
